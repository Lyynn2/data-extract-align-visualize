Calibrate IMU : Left up  , toggle Right side to side
Calibrate gyro: Left down, toggle Right side to side
Calibrate comp: toggle mode switch

Drone will not unlock if weak GPS and in GPS mode - use ATTI mode (attitude mode)

Minimum takeoff altitude seems to be 50cm

-----------------------------------------------------
MANUAL CONTROL
-----------------------------------------------------
Unlock with controller: both joysticks down and in
Left: ascend/descend, turn
Right: move
Smooth+: left yaw, right roll (press button then use knob)
Land/lock with controller: descend, then hold left down or move both down and out
